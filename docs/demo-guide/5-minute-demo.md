# 5 minute demo

## 5 minute demo

### Goal

Show that TITAN creates a treasury, executes a workflow, and produces verifiable proof.

### Flow

1. Open the app and connect a wallet.
2. Show treasury context and funding state.
3. Show an obligation or workflow execution path.
4. Open Proof Center and verify the resulting digest.
5. Close on portfolio and audit visibility.
