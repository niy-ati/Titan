# DefiLlama

## DefiLlama

### What it is

DefiLlama is TITAN’s cross-protocol yield and TVL source.

### Why it exists

It provides a broad market baseline for lending and LP opportunity discovery.

### How it works

The Liquidity Terminal consumes pool and protocol-level metrics, then normalizes them into deployment views.

### Where it lives

* Liquidity Terminal data layer
* market and opportunity tables
